University of Idaho Faculty Profile

 

Dr. Amin Mirkouei is an Assistant Professor in In Industrial Technology and Technology Management at the University of Idaho  in Idaho Falls, where he directs the Intelligent Design and Manufacturing Laboratory. Dr. Mirkouei was recruited by Georgia Southern University (GSU) as a Visiting Assistant Professor immediately after he obtained his Ph.D. Before his faculty assignment at GSU, Dr. Mirkouei served as a Graduate Research/Teaching Assistant (GRA/GTA), Lab Instructor, and Course Instructor in the School of Mechanical, Industrial, and Manufacturing Engineering within the College of Engineering at Oregon State University while pursuing his doctoral degree. Dr. Mirkouei holds a Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering with a minor in Business Administration and concentration in Manufacturing Systems Engineering from OSU and a M.S. degree also in Industrial Engineering from the University of Tehran, Iran. Before pursuing his two advanced degrees, he obtained his B.S. in Industrial Engineering from Islamic Azad University in Iran.

As listed in his Curriculum Vitae, his considerable portfolio of academic papers, conference presentations, reviewer invitations by scholarly journals, and his membership in prestigious professional organizations, such as, ASME, IISE, SME, evidently indicate his wide recognition within the scientific sphere.
