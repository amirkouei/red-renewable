Professional Experience

  • 2017-~ Tenure-Track Assistant Professor, Department of Nuclear Engineering and Industrial Management, University of Idaho (UI), ID
  • 2016-17 Visiting Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Georgia Southern University (GSU), GA
  • 2014-16 Instructor, School of Mechanical, Industrial, and Manufacturing Engineering, Oregon State University (OSU), OR
  • 2012-16 Graduate Research and Teaching Assistant, School of Mechanical, Industrial, and Manufacturing Engineering, OSU, OR
  • 2014 Graduate Researcher, Microproducts Breakthrough Institute, Corvallis, OR

 

Areas of Specialization

  • Technology Development: thermochemical (fast and slow pyrolysis) process, water treatment process for removing oversupply nutrients (phosphorus and ammonia), and agromining process for e-waste recycling and rare earth elements recovery
  • Product Development: bio-oil, biochar, and green chemicals (furfural)
  • Sustainability Assessment: life cycle assessment (LCA) and techno-economic assessment (TEA)
  • Biofuels Conversion Processes and Modeling: thermochemical, electrochemical, and ultrasonic cavitation
  • Smart Design and Manufacturing: cyber-physical monitoring, control, and optimization
  • Operations Research and Management: logistics and supply chain optimization
